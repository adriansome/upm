  • The Avalon Marshes

    The Avalon Marshes is an ancient wetland landscape in the heart of Somerset, significant for its hidden cultural heritage as well as its huge range of wildlife. Home to ancient Neolithic trackways, stunning wildflower meadows, and thousands of wintering wildfowl, this landscape has many ancient and modern stories to tell and reveal.
